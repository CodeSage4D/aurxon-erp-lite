# School ERP – Phase II Requirements and Enhancements

## Overview

Phase II should focus on ensuring smooth system operations, fixing all existing bugs, improving usability, and making the ERP fully production-ready. Based on the current assessment, Phase I is not yet completely deployment-ready and requires further refinements.

In addition to bug fixes, several new modules, fields, workflows, and role-based functionalities should be added to support the complete academic and administrative operations of the school.

---

* Manage certifications and training records.
